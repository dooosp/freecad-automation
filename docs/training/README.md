# Training Modules

These materials package the repo as a beginner-friendly DELMIA-adjacent manufacturing DX learning prototype. They are not official DELMIA training and they do not claim 3DEXPERIENCE platform integration.

## Modules

- [Module 01: Digital Manufacturing](./module-01-digital-manufacturing.md)
- [Module 02: Virtual Twin Basics](./module-02-virtual-twin-basics.md)
- [Module 03: MES, APS, and SCM Overview](./module-03-mes-aps-scm-overview.md)
- [Module 04: AI-Assisted Manufacturing Review](./module-04-ai-assisted-manufacturing-review.md)
- [Demo Script](./demo-script-freecad-automation.md)

## Intended Audience

- Manufacturing engineers
- Solution consultants
- Partner enablement teams
- Beginner-friendly technical stakeholders

## Delivery Suggestion

- 60 to 90 minute workshop
- 15 minutes of concept framing
- 25 minutes of live demo
- 20 minutes of discussion and objections handling
