# Demo Case Study Template

Use this template for portfolio-friendly demo scenarios. Do not invent real customer claims.

## Industry

Describe the demo industry and product family.

## Business Problem

Describe the operational pain point in plain language.

## Data Sources

- CAD or configuration input
- BOM or supply context
- Routing or line assumptions
- Inspection or quality evidence
- Runtime or launch data if relevant

## Virtual Twin / Simulation Angle

Describe how a DELMIA-adjacent virtual-twin-style review could help before physical changes are made.

## AI Opportunity

Describe what review acceleration, summarization, triage, or recommendation support is plausible.

## Expected KPI Impact

List 2 to 4 KPI areas such as throughput, WIP, FPY, rework, lead time, changeover, or launch stability.

## How This Repo Can Demonstrate A Simplified Version

Point to specific docs, configs, schemas, or demo scripts in this repository.

## Safe Positioning

End with a reminder that the scenario is a demo narrative, not an official customer result or DELMIA integration claim.
